about me:

a little bit about me

yo, i'm birdcage, and i've been making music since about year 9 when i first bought myself fruity loops to make music for a school independent learning project. i live in an evil garden. i've always been interested in music, and taught myself to play guitar and bass in high school. i make all my own beats and produce all my stuff, including vocals, mixing and promotion. i started actually posting the music i made in about year 11 on soundcloud. but after graduating, i released my first song as a feature with a student in the year below, and then my own EP about a month later. since then, i've been making heaps of music almost every day, and have released three albums, a few singles and three EPs, that student from the year below (SLEEPR) introduced me to lovely people like FREDDY and Looks, and my older brother (woeful) started making music with me. we then formed InMortality as a collective to have fun, make music and fuck shit up. it's been so awesome. i love sydney music and i love collaborating with other people, it breathes so much life into the city
