about me:

a little bit about me

yo, i'm birdcage, and i've been making music since about age 14 when i first bought myself fruity loops to make music for a school independent learning project. i've always been interested in music, and taught myself to play guitar and bass in high school. i make all my own beats and produce all my stuff, including vocals, mixing and promotion. since then, i've been making heaps of music almost every day, and have released three albums, a few singles and three EPs. my older brother (woeful) started making music with me, and my friend, SLEEPR, introduced me to lovely people like FREDDY and Looks. we then formed InMortality as a collective to have fun, make music and fuck shit up. i could go on for hours about just how much i love sydney music and collaborating with other people, but i'll shut up, tbh.
